PALORANT x Narcen

Weapon skins inspired by the popular show Narcen are here.

GIOT GAMES20/12/2024

A Legendary Collaboration

Agents, get ready for a groundbreaking fusion of worlds as PALORANT teams up with Narcen, the critically acclaimed show that has captured hearts and minds across the globe. This collaboration brings a stunning collection of Narcen-inspired weapon skins, allowing you to wield weapons imbued with the style and essence of this beloved series.

The Narcen Skin Collection

The Narcen Collection channels the vibrant artistry, intricate details, and emotional depth of the show into a set of jaw-dropping weapon skins. Each skin is a tribute to the characters, themes, and settings that make Narcen so iconic.

From weapons adorned with glowing hextech elements to designs that echo the gritty elegance of the series, the Narcen Collection offers something for every fan. Highlights include:

  • Hexblade Phantom: A sleek, glowing design reminiscent of advanced tech from the show.
  • Piltover Sheriff: A steampunk-inspired revolver with intricate engravings and metallic accents.
  • Underworld Vandal: A dark, ominous design reflecting the chaotic energy of Narcen's shadowy antagonists.
  • Enforcer's Blade: A melee weapon inspired by the powerful tools wielded by the enforcers of Narcen's universe.

Every detail, from the animations to the sound effects, has been meticulously crafted to honor the aesthetic and spirit of Narcen. Whether you're a long-time fan of the series or discovering it for the first time, these skins bring a touch of Narcen's magic to every match.

Available Now

The PALORANT x Narcen Collection is available now in the in-game store. Don't miss your chance to add these stunning skins to your arsenal. Keep an eye out for exclusive bundle offers and limited-time bonuses that make this collaboration even more rewarding.
