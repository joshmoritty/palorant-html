Agent Reveal: Teko

I'm gonna make you an offer you can't refuse.

AGENT REVEALGIOT GAMES03/01/2025

An Offer You Can't Refuse

Agents, the PALORANT Protocol has a new wildcard—meet Teko, a cunning initiator with a penchant for manipulation and control. Inspired by the underworld's calculated power plays, Teko’s abilities are designed to sow chaos, mislead opponents, and create opportunities for their team to capitalize on. This shadowy operator is here to change the game, one clever move at a time.

A Deceptive Skillset

Teko’s abilities focus on disrupting enemy plans and controlling the flow of engagements. Here’s a closer look at what they bring to the battlefield:

  • Smoke Bargain: Deploy a deceptive smoke that expands and contracts erratically, obscuring vision and disorienting enemies within it. Use it to block sightlines or confuse opponents in critical moments.
  • Marked Deal: Throw a tracking device that reveals enemy locations when it sticks to surfaces or players, acting as a constant thorn in their side.
  • Trade Secrets: Place a decoy that mimics Teko’s movements for a limited time. Enemies who shoot the decoy are briefly revealed on the map, exposing their position.
  • Ultimate: Blackout Market: Create a localized area where enemy abilities are swapped randomly, leaving them scrambling to adapt to unfamiliar tools while your team strikes.

Teko’s kit rewards creative players who thrive on mind games, deception, and outsmarting their opponents.

The Mastermind’s Backstory

Hailing from a background of underground dealings and power struggles, Teko rose through the ranks by outthinking and outmaneuvering everyone in their path. With a mind for strategy and an aura of charisma, they’ve learned to play the long game, leveraging every situation to their advantage.

After being recruited by the PALORANT Protocol, Teko now uses their skills for a greater cause, but their knack for bending the rules and crafting “offers you can’t refuse” remains as sharp as ever.

Changing the Meta

Teko brings a fresh take on how teams approach engagements. Their abilities encourage bold plays, misdirection, and turning the enemy’s strategy against them. Whether you’re baiting opponents into a trap, gathering intel, or disorienting foes, Teko opens up countless new possibilities for creative strategies.

Ready to Make a Deal?

Teko is now live in PALORANT! Take them into the battlefield and experience their unique approach to combat and control. As always, we can’t wait to see the incredible plays and strategies you create with this new agent.

The question is: will you outsmart your opponents or fall victim to Teko’s cunning tactics? The choice is yours—if you dare.
