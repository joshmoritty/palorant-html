Patch Notes #3.0

Teko joins the team, new skins, new maps, and more.

GAME UPDATESGIOT GAMES07/01/2025

Welcome to Patch 3.0

Agents, the battlefield is evolving once again with the release of Patch 3.0, one of our most exciting updates yet. This patch introduces the enigmatic new agent Teko, alongside stunning skins, dynamic maps, and a host of gameplay improvements. Let's dive into the highlights of this update!

Teko Joins the Team

The star of Patch 3.0 is none other than Teko, the newest initiator to join the PALORANT roster. Teko's unique abilities center around deception, control, and disrupting enemy plans. From deploying decoys to creating chaos with their ultimate Blackout Market, Teko brings a fresh twist to the tactical gameplay you know and love.

New Skins: Underworld Elegance Collection

The Underworld Elegance Collection debuts in this patch, bringing a sleek and mysterious aesthetic inspired by high-stakes deals and shadowy alliances. These skins combine intricate designs with glowing accents to make every weapon feel like an artifact of power.

The collection includes:

  • Wraith Phantom: A sleek, smoke-gray design with pulsing red highlights.
  • Cobra Sheriff: A venomous look with scales and shimmering green effects.
  • Vendetta Vandal: A weapon that glows with a dark aura, radiating an aura of control.
  • Ace's Blade: A deadly melee weapon with a razor-sharp, polished edge.

Equip these skins and strike fear into your enemies with both style and precision.

New Maps: Flakor and Binti

This patch also introduces two new maps, each offering unique challenges and opportunities:

Flakor: Set in a bustling, neon-lit city, this map combines narrow alleyways with wide open spaces perfect for ambushes and tactical plays. Verticality is key here, as players can access rooftops to gain an advantage.

Binti: A desolate, post-apocalyptic arena featuring sprawling dunes, crumbling structures, and limited cover. Use the environment to your advantage in this map's dynamic, high-risk engagements.

Both maps are now part of the competitive and unranked rotations, so jump in and explore!

Gameplay Improvements and Fixes

In addition to new content, Patch 3.0 brings several quality-of-life updates and balance changes:

  • Agent Tweaks: Adjustments to improve balance for several agents, including buffs for Phoenix and nerfs to Chamber.
  • Ranked Updates: Improved matchmaking to ensure fairer matches and better progression through the ranks.
  • Audio Enhancements: Updated sound design for better spatial awareness in-game.
  • Bug Fixes: Resolved issues with map clipping, ability hitboxes, and server stability.

We've also implemented general optimizations to ensure smoother performance across all platforms.

See You on the Battlefield

That's all for Patch 3.0! Log in now to experience the new content and updates. Gear up, strategize, and remember: the battlefield is yours to command. Good luck out there, Agents!
