PALORANT is Coming to Console!

PALORANT will release on consoles on February 31st 2025.

GIOT GAMES15/12/2024

The Wait is Over, Console Players!

Agents, we've heard your requests loud and clear. After countless hours of development and optimization, we are thrilled to announce that PALORANT is officially coming to consoles! Mark your calendars—PALORANT will launch on consoles on February 31st, 2025.

Experience PALORANT Like Never Before

Our goal has always been to make PALORANT accessible to as many players as possible while maintaining the competitive integrity and precision gameplay you've come to love. Bringing PALORANT to consoles is a massive step in that direction. Console players can now immerse themselves in the same thrilling battles, tactical gameplay, and intense moments that PC players have been enjoying.

Optimized for Console Gameplay

We've made significant efforts to ensure PALORANT feels just as fluid and competitive on consoles as it does on PC. Here are some key features you can expect:

  • Controller Support: Intuitive and responsive controls tailored specifically for gamepads.
  • Crossplay: Enable or disable crossplay options to match your preferred playstyle and competition level.
  • Performance Enhancements: Optimized graphics and frame rates to ensure smooth gameplay across all major console platforms.

Rest assured, we've worked tirelessly to ensure that console players have an experience that's both accessible and competitive.

The Journey to Console

Bringing PALORANT to consoles has been a long-anticipated milestone for the Giot Games team. We've taken the time to listen to the community, test extensively, and refine every aspect of the console experience. The result? A version of PALORANT that retains the core elements of the game while adapting seamlessly to the console ecosystem.

Get Ready to Deploy

With the release date fast approaching, now is the time to gear up and prepare for the fight. Whether you're a seasoned PC player or brand-new to PALORANT, this console launch is the perfect opportunity to join the action. We can't wait to welcome a whole new wave of agents to the battlefield!

Stay tuned for more updates, including pre-order details, beta access information, and exclusive console-specific content. As always, thank you for your support, passion, and dedication. We'll see you on February 31st, 2025—ready up, Agents!
